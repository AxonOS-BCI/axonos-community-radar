The plain terms

  • Contributions are voluntary. They are not purchases, investments, securities, governance rights, token allocations or promises of financial return.
  • No product entitlement is granted for voluntary contributions — everything here is already free for everyone.
  • Crypto transactions are irreversible. Verify the complete address before sending. Network fees and market volatility are the sender's responsibility.
  • AxonOS will never ask for your wallet seed phrase, private key or remote wallet access.
  • Commercial licence payments (e.g. for the Neural Boundary Game's commercial licence) require a written agreement and invoice; fiat (SGD or USD) is the default. See CRYPTO_PAYMENT_TERMS.md or write to connect@axonos.org.
